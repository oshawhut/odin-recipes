Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

This pasta dish is an Italian staple and one of my favorite meals to eat growing up! Fettuccine Alfredo is a creamy and indulgent pasta dish. It starts with long, flat noodles called fettuccine, which are cooked until they're soft and tender. Then, they're coated in a rich and creamy sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and grated Parmesan cheese. The sauce is often seasoned with a bit of garlic and some black pepper for flavor. It's a comforting and decadent meal that many people enjoy, especially if they love creamy and cheesy pasta dishes. It's like a warm and cozy hug for your taste buds! ğŸğŸ˜‹

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Melt butter into cream in a large saucepan over low heat; add salt, pepper, and garlic salt. Increase the heat to medium; stir in grated Romano and Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.
  3. Add cooked pasta to sauce and toss until thoroughly coated; serve immediately.
